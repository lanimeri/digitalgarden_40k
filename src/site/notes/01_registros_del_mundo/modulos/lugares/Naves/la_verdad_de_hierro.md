## Detalles Adicionales 

Tipo de nave: Orca-Class
These warships, built by Romanov Shipyards in New Alghero, were initially meant to be merchantmen, massive freighters. However, these were converted to battleships, and space meant for crops became space meant for cannon and shells. These vessels are extremely well-armed, with enormous energy cannons and 3 MACs. AAA systems line the decks, making the vessels extremely well-equipped against fighter and bomber opponents. However, these vessels carry little armor, and engine systems are considered slower than the norm. These vessels are considered the working-man of the Navy.
